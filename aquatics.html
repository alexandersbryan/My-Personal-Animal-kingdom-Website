All about our Aquatics:

When we got into the hobby: 2018

How it started: 1 betta fish in a gallon bowl

How it ended: 200 gal tank with various predatory species

Fish: Cleaner shrimp, Oscellaris Clown fish, Yellow tang, Foxface Rabbitfish(venomous), Flame angelfish, Solar fairy Wrasse, and sand sifting starfish

Corals: Pulsing xenias, GSP(Green star polyps), Lord Acans, Yellow Zoanthids(poisionous), Hammer Corals, Torch Corals, Kryptonite Candy Cane Corals

Favorite food: Mysis shrimp, seaweed, flakes

Recommended Hobbiest level: Advanced
